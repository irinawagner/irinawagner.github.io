ABOUT THE PROJECT

The Arapaho Language Project is a website created by the students and faculty of the University of Colorado at Boulder to assist language revitalization of the Arapaho language. In the last couple of years, the content and the design of the website have become outdated and do not fully respond to the needs of the community. Along with another graduate student and a faculty member, I have helped to update the design of the website and some of its language learning contents. As of March 2015, the website had gone live.
